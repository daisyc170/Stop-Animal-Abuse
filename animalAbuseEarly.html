How to take care of pets at home?

Some examples of how to take care of pets are feeding your pets, please don't ignore them when they are hungry. If your pet is a dog, please take them out for a long walk if you can but at least half an hour is required. Of course pets get dirty, its important to keep them clean and especially have a clean and hygenic environment for them. Pets also need engagement, please do not leave them alone for a long time, they also get lonely and sad!
